1. Why Choose Air-Purifying Plants?

Nature has a built-in air filter.
It's green, silent, and smells amazing.

Air-purifying plants help:

  • Remove toxins like formaldehyde and benzene
  • Boost oxygen levels
  • Regulate humidity
  • Reduce dust and airborne microbes

2. How Plants Improve Your Mood

Plants don't just clean the air.
They calm your nervous system.

Psychological benefits include:

  • Reduced stress and anxiety
  • Improved concentration and memory
  • Enhanced creativity
  • Elevated mood and sense of well-being

Just looking at greenery can lower cortisol levels.
