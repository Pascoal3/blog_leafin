4. Step-by-Step: How to Build a Vertical Garden

Step 1: Choose the Location

Step 2: Set Up the Structure

  • Mount a pallet, trellis, or frame against the wall
  • Ensure stability and weight support
  • Add waterproof backing if installing indoors

Step 3: Arrange Your Planters

  • Use uniform spacing to allow airflow
  • Place sun-loving plants higher, shade-lovers lower
  • Ensure each container has drainage holes

Step 4: Add Soil and Plants

  • Fill with rich, lightweight soil
  • Transplant seedlings or sow seeds
  • Water gently but thoroughly

Step 5: Maintain Regularly

  • Trim dead leaves and rotate crops if needed
