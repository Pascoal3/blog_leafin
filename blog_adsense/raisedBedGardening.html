4. Step-by-Step Building Instructions

Step 1: Plan the Size

Step 2: Cut and Assemble the Frame

  • Cut your planks to desired lengths
  • Arrange them in a rectangle
  • Attach corners with screws or brackets
  • Use a level to ensure the frame is even

Place the frame where you want the garden to stay.

Step 3: Prepare the Ground

  • Remove grass or weeds
  • Lay down cardboard or landscape fabric to block future growth
  • This layer will also help with drainage and reduce pests

Step 4: Fill with Soil

Use a high-quality mix:

  • 60% topsoil
  • 30% compost
  • 10% perlite or sand (for drainage)

Fill to the top, then gently water and let settle overnight.

Step 5: Start Planting!

Now the fun begins, add seeds or seedlings based on your garden plan.

Tips:

  • Use square-foot gardening for organization
  • Plant taller crops on the north side
  • Mulch the surface to retain moisture

5. Maintenance and Tips

  • Use row covers to protect young plants
  • Rotate crops annually to avoid soil fatigue
